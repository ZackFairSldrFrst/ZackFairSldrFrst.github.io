About Us

Welcome to Skinimalism, where we believe in enhancing your skin's natural beauty through effective, personalized facial treatments. Our spa is dedicated to providing a tranquil environment where you can relax and rejuvenate while receiving expert skincare services.

At Skinimalism, we focus on techniques and products that work with your skin's natural processes, not against them. Our approach combines proven skincare science with luxurious, pampering treatments to give you the best results possible.

Located in Burnaby, BC, our spa offers a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Come experience the Skinimalism difference today.
