Our Story

Passion for culinary excellence

Founded in 2010, our restaurant began with a simple mission: to create memorable dining experiences through exceptional food, attentive service, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Our chef-driven cuisine focuses on seasonal ingredients sourced from local farmers and producers. We believe in supporting our community while delivering the freshest flavors to your table.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying an evening out, we're committed to making every visit remarkable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Do you accept reservations?
Yes, we highly recommend making reservations, especially for dinner and weekends. You can reserve a table online through our website or by calling us directly.
What is your dress code?
We have a smart casual dress code. While formal attire is not required, we do ask that guests refrain from wearing athletic wear, flip-flops, or overly casual clothing.
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Absolutely! We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please inform us of any allergies or dietary restrictions when making your reservation so our chefs can prepare accordingly.
Is there parking available?
We offer complimentary valet parking for our dinner guests. There is also a public parking garage one block away.
Do you host private events?
Yes, we have a private dining room that can accommodate up to 30 guests for special occasions, corporate events, or celebrations. Please contact our events coordinator for details and availability.
