About JE Notaries

JE Notaries is a professional partnership between Jeffrey Lam Notary Public Inc. and Elaine Lam Notary Corporation, united by a shared commitment to excellence, integrity, and client-focused service.

Who We Are

With over 17 years of combined experience, Jeffrey and Elaine Lam bring a unique perspective to their practice.

Jeffrey Lam

Jeffrey's previous background as a Chartered Professional Accountant (CPA) equips him with both the accounting and legal knowledge to offer clients deep expertise when navigating through complex transactions.

Elaine Lam

Elaine Lam brings valuable experience from the banking and accounting industries, paired with a strong focus on detail, clarity, and personalized service.

Together, JE Notaries provides reliable and efficient legal services, including real estate transactions, wills, powers of attorney, and more — all delivered with integrity, professionalism, and care.
