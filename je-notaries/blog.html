The Importance of Updated Estate Planning Documents

Life changes constantly, and your estate planning documents should reflect these changes. Many people create wills and powers of attorney but forget to update them as their circumstances evolve.

Major life events such as marriage, divorce, birth of children, significant asset acquisitions, or changes in family relationships all warrant a review of your estate planning documents. Regular updates ensure your wishes are clearly documented and legally enforceable.
