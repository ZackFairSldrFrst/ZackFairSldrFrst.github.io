Welcome to the Work Back Schedule Calculator!

Plan your project by working backward from the end date. Follow these steps to create your customized schedule:

  1. Enter Project End Date: Choose the date you plan to finish your project, e.g., product launch date.
  2. Add Your Tasks: List the tasks needed for your project, such as market research, content creation, and design.
  3. Customize Your Schedule: Exclude weekends, skip statutory holidays, and set your location.
  4. Hit "Calculate Schedule": Your task dates will be calculated instantly, aiding in project planning.
  5. Suggested Kick-off Date: See the recommended project kick-off date at the end.

Welcome to the Forward Schedule Calculator!

Plan your project from the start date and find out when it will finish. Follow these steps:

  1. Enter Project Start Date: Choose the date when you want to begin your project.
  2. Add Your Tasks: List the tasks and estimate their durations, e.g., campaign planning, content creation, and execution.
  3. Customize Your Schedule: Exclude weekends, skip statutory holidays, and set your location.
  4. Hit "Calculate Schedule": Your task dates and earliest possible finish date will be calculated.
