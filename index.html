Welcome to the Work Back Schedule Calculator!

Planning your project just got easier. Follow these steps to create your customized schedule:

  1. Enter Project End Date: Start by selecting the date you plan to finish your project.
  2. Add Your Tasks: List the tasks needed for your project and estimate their durations.
  3. Customize Your Schedule: Choose options to exclude weekends, skip statutory holidays, and set your location.
  4. Hit "Calculate Schedule": Watch the magic happen! Your task dates will be calculated instantly.
  5. Suggested Kick-off Date: See the recommended project kick-off date at the end.

Ready to get started? Let's make your project planning a breeze!

Welcome to the Forward Schedule Calculator!

Plan your project from the start date and find out when it will finish. Follow these steps:

  3. Customize Your Schedule: Exclude weekends, skip statutory holidays, and set your location.
  4. Hit "Calculate Schedule": Your task dates and earliest possible finish date will be calculated.
