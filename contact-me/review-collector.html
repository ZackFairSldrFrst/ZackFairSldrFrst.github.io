Frequently Asked Questions

How will my review be used?

Your review may be displayed on my website, social media, or marketing materials, depending on the permissions you grant. I value your privacy and will only use your review in the ways you've approved.

Can I update or remove my review later?

Yes, you can update or request removal of your review at any time. Simply contact me with your request, and I'll take care of it promptly.

Are all reviews published?

I publish all authentic reviews, both positive and constructive. I believe in transparency and value all feedback. However, I reserve the right to not publish reviews containing inappropriate content.

How can I feature my success story?

If you'd like to share a more detailed success story about our work together, please mention this in your review or contact me directly. I'd be happy to discuss featuring your project as a case study.
