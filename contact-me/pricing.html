Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! Our Pro plan comes with a 7-day free trial with no credit card required. You can explore all the features and see if it's right for you before making a commitment.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. If you upgrade, you'll be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.

How does the agency plan work?

The agency plan allows for up to 5 agent accounts under a single subscription. Each agent gets their own profile and pages, but they can be managed centrally. For larger teams, contact our sales team for custom pricing.

Can I use my own domain name?

Yes, Pro and Agency plans allow you to connect your own custom domain to your agent page. We provide simple instructions to set this up with your domain registrar.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. For agency plans, we can also arrange for invoice payment.
