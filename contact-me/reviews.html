Frequently Asked Questions

How do I send review requests to clients?

You can send review requests via email or text message directly from your dashboard. The system allows you to personalize messages and schedule them to be sent automatically after closing.

Can I customize the review form?

Yes! You can customize the form with your branding, photo, and add additional questions specific to your business.

How do I display reviews on my website?

We provide simple embed codes for adding review widgets to your website. You can choose from different display styles and filter which reviews to show.

Can I respond to reviews?

Absolutely! You can respond to reviews directly from your dashboard, and your responses will be displayed alongside the original review.
